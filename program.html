Our Programs

Child protection and inclusion
Every child has the right to grow up in
a safe and inclusive environment

We work with partners around the Continent(Africa) to promote policies
and expand access to services that
protect all children.

Child survival
Every child has the right to survive and thrive

We helped reduce child mortality all
over the world by working to reach the
most vulnerable children, everywhere.

Social policy
Every child has the right to an equitable chance in life

We work around the world to reduce
child poverty and shield girls and boys
from its lifelong consequences.

Innovation for children
Innovating to drive results for
every child

TAFGM works with partners in every
sector to co-create innovative solutions
that accelerate progress for children and young people.

Supply and logistics
Delivering solutions to reach every child
at risk

We deliver sustainable access to
lifesaving supplies where they are most needed, accelerating results for the most vulnerable children.

Gender
Every girl has the right to fulfil her potential

Together Against FGM(TAFGM) works all over the world to empower girls and
women, and to ensure their full
participation in political, social, and economic systems.

Together Against FGM is an organisation to help the girl child and women at large to end the belief and concept likewise the practice of FGM.
